Chemistry Department

Department Information

The department of chemistry was established in 1950. The department deals with curriculum in applied chemistry / material science common to all branches in engineering. The department thus only deals with first year student is treated as minor one.
Besides teaching activity the staff members of this department are involved in teaching of some materials like alloy, cement, lime, alum, bleaching powder, water and aggregates. Also coordinate first year admission process & other activities of the collage.
In the last five years the faculty members of the department have contributed more than 30 research papers in international journals of repute and international conference proceedings.
